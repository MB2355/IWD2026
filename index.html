The Future is Global

This event aims to highlight how makerspaces and hands-on learning environments empower women to become creators rather than just consumers of technology.

By sharing real stories and experiences, the webinar seeks to encourage more girls and young women to explore fields such as engineering, design, digital fabrication, entrepreneurship, and research.

"Here's to strong women. May we know them. May we be them. May we raise them."
